Utility 1 is a 2019 Ford Expedition built by Bobby Jones Ford of Augusta, GA. It has a EcoBoost turbo V6 engine and a 10-speed automatic transmission. Utility 1 is a multi-purpose unit used by on-duty part time staff and used during medical calls, mutual aid calls, training, travel and other duties.

Apparatus Inventory

 

Engine 4 is a 2003 Pierce pumper/tanker mounted on a Kenworth chassis, with a Cummins 330 diesel engine and an Allison 5-speed automatic transmission. It has a 1250 GPM Waterous pump with a Hercules/Foam Pro CAF system and carries 1000 gallons of water with two foam tanks that hold 20 gallons of foam each. This unit also has a deluge gun, hydraulic ladder rack and hydraulic-driven generator with cord reel. Engine 4 is the first out apparatus from Station 2 on all incidents in the eastern part of our district except for wildland fires.

Rescue 7 is a 2000 Pierce mounted on a Freightliner chassis, with a Cummins 300 diesel engine and an Allison 5-speed automatic transmission. This unit is equipped with a generator that powers an electric line reel and an electric-powered hydraulic pump, which in turn powers two hydraulic line reels. Also, an onboard air compressor delivers air to an air line reel. This unit carries an extensive list of equipment used for vehicle, farm and machinery extrication. Rescue 7 is the second out apparatus on all rescue incidents in the district, responding with a first out engine from either Station 1 or Station 2.

Brush 2 is a 1995 Dodge Ram 3500 4X4 built by Anchor-Richey EVS. It has a Cummins turbo diesel engine with a 4-speed automatic transmission. The skid unit is equipped with a 200 GPM Hale pump powered by a Briggs & Stratton 18 hp engine, 300 gallon water tank, 10 gallon Class A foam tank, a Blizzard-Wizard foam inductor and a booster reel mounted on top. This unit also has a 1.5 in. pre-connect line and 1 in. "forest service" line. Brush 2 is the first out apparatus from Station 2 on all wildland fires in the eastern part of our district.
