Our Mission

The Salem Fire and Rescue Protection Association was established by the residents of the Salem Community to provide residents and visitors with lifesaving, fire and rescue protection services through the Association's fire and rescue department.

The Salem Fire and Rescue Department provides the Salem Community with fire protection, first responder and rescue services. In addition, the department is committed to offering other services which are in keeping with its protection of life and property mandate.

The Salem Fire and Rescue Department will also provide mutual aid to neighboring departments and communities - in keeping with formal agreements - and support the county and other North Carolina emergency response agencies with disaster assistance.
